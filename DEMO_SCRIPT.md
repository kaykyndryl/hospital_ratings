# Hospital Ratings Longitudinal Analysis - Demo Script

## Opening
"Welcome to the Hospital Ratings Longitudinal Analysis system. This app transforms Medicare hospital quality data into actionable insights for healthcare administrators. Instead of looking at a single snapshot, we analyze five years of historical data to identify trends, forecast future performance, and recommend evidence-based improvements."

---

## Section 1: Navigation & Hospital Selection
"Let's start by selecting a hospital. I'm going to pick [Hospital Name] from the dropdown. As you can see, we're pulling from a dataset of 120 hospitals with 5 years of historical data."

---

## Section 2: Performance Tab (Current Year Overview)
"The **Performance** tab gives you the current snapshot. Here you can see:
- Overall quality rating
- Key metrics: mortality rates, readmission rates, safety measures like CLABSI
- How this hospital compares to state and national benchmarks
- Whether they're performing above or below average"

---

## Section 3: Trends Tab (5-Year History)
"Now let's switch to the **Trends** tab. This is where things get interesting. Over the past 5 years, we can see:
- The trajectory of each metric
- Is mortality trending up or down?
- Is readmission improving?
- We're tracking 1-year, 3-year, and 5-year changes so you can see if improvements are recent or sustained

*Point to specific trend lines* These arrows and percentages tell the story of performance evolution."

---

## Section 4: Projections Tab (1-Year Forecast)
"The **Projections** tab uses historical trends to forecast the next year. Based on current momentum:
- If this metric is improving at 2% per year, where will it be in 12 months?
- This helps administrators plan interventions—do we need to act now to prevent decline, or can we maintain current trajectory?
- These projections are based on linear regression from actual historical data."

---

## Section 5: Impact Actions Tab (AI-Enhanced Recommendations)
"The **Impact Actions** tab is the core of what makes this system valuable. These aren't generic recommendations—they're:

1. **Ranked by estimated impact** - We calculate how much improvement each action could deliver
2. **Evidence-based** - Each recommendation shows peer hospitals that have achieved similar results
3. **Quantified** - You see not just 'improve readmission rates' but '8-15% improvement is achievable' with confidence levels
4. **Actionable** - Including implementation timeframe and cost-benefit context

*Select an action* Here's an example: If we reduce CLABSI rates through infection prevention protocols, peer data shows hospitals achieve [10-18%] improvement in [6-9] months."

---

## Section 6: Comparison Tab (Peer Analysis)
"The **Comparison** tab lets you put this hospital against peers. You can compare:
- Side-by-side metrics with other hospitals
- Regional trends
- Which hospitals are moving in similar directions
- This helps identify best performers to learn from"

---

## Section 7: AI Insights (OpenRouter Integration)
"Behind the scenes, we've integrated OpenRouter's GPT-4 model to add another layer of intelligence. When enabled, the system:
- Analyzes the full hospital profile
- Generates executive summaries
- Identifies key insights hidden in the data
- Prioritizes improvement opportunities with contextual reasoning
- Provides implementation guidance

*If AI is active, show the AI Insights panel* These insights combine our rule-based algorithms with advanced language models for richer analysis."

---

## Section 8: Data Scope & Year Selection
"You can adjust the analysis window with the year range selector at the top. Want to analyze the last 3 years instead of 5? The app recalculates all trends, projections, and peer comparisons instantly. This flexibility lets you focus on different time horizons depending on your questions."

---

## Closing
"This system transforms raw CMS data into a strategic tool for hospital leadership. Instead of asking 'What's our rating?' administrators can ask 'Where are we heading?' and 'What specific actions will move us forward?' The combination of historical trend analysis, peer benchmarking, and AI-generated insights creates a comprehensive picture for decision-making."

---

## Demo Tips

- **Start with a high-performer**: Shows what good trends look like
- **Then show a hospital with mixed results**: Demonstrates how projections and impact actions help prioritize
- **Highlight the peer evidence**: This is unique—showing actual numbers from similar hospitals is compelling
- **Use the comparison feature**: Side-by-side comparison resonates with administrators
- **Point out the confidence levels**: Showing uncertainty ranges builds credibility
- **Mention the 5-year span**: This differentiates from typical annual reporting
